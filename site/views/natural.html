    In order to use this program:
  • Press 'f' to get the Findlay Notes for this section

  • Press 'right arrow' to move to the next word.
  • Press 'left arrow' ...
  • Press 'w' to lookup the summary of the term on wikipedia.
  • Press 'a' to lookup some of the entries for the word on the Wordnet Database

  • If you get a bug, press 'x' and then 'e' or 'q' to move to another section.
